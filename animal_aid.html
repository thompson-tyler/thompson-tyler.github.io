ANIMAL AID PAGE UNDER CONSTRUCTION

My first project after joining JumboCode was with the Animal Aid team. Animal Aid is a student organization which connects students walkers to local dogs who need to be walked. This laborous process of matching students and dogs was always done manually by comparing dozens of spreadsheets containing student and dog schedules, limiting their potential scale. Our custom platform would allow students to log in and fill out their schedule, contact info, and select their dog preferences, while the Animal Aid staff populates the site with dog profiles. And once the signup deadline rolls around, staff just need to press a single button to run our matching algorithm, populating student profiles with their walking schedule. We worked closely with out client and deployed a site to their spec within five months of development.

Django, Python, PostgreSQL, AWS (S3), JavaScript, HTML
